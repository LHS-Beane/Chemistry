Core Relationships

Avogadro's Number

1 mole = 6.022 × 1023 particles

Use this when converting between moles and particles.

Molar Mass

molar mass = grams per mole

Use the periodic table to convert between moles and grams.

Conversion Map

Particles ↔ Moles ↔ Grams

Every conversion goes through moles.

Particles → Moles

Divide by 6.022 × 1023

Moles → Particles

Multiply by 6.022 × 1023

Moles ↔ Grams

Use molar mass from the periodic table

Specific Atoms in a Formula

To find a specific atom inside a compound, first find the number of molecules or formula units, then multiply by the subscript.

Key Idea

In H2O:

  • 2 hydrogen atoms per molecule
  • 1 oxygen atom per molecule

Flow

Grams → Moles → Molecules → Specific Atoms

Always multiply by the subscript at the end.

Specific Atom Example 1
