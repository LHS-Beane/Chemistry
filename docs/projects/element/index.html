Mission Deliverables

There are three phases to your campaign. Click to explore requirements.

20 Points The Executive Summary

Before you can sell it, you have to understand it. You will write a formal research report covering four key sections.

Section A: The ID Card

  • Name, Symbol, Atomic #
  • Average Atomic Mass
  • Proton, Neutron, Electron count
  • Electron Config & Location (Group/Period)

Section B: Personality Profile

  • Physical: State, density, melting point, color
  • Chemical: Reactivity, electronegativity
  • Isotopes: Major ones & radioactivity

Section C: The Resume (Uses)

  • History: Who discovered it and when?
  • 3 Distinct Uses: Medicine, Tech, etc.
  • Constraint: Explain WHY it is used chemically.
  • Biology: Essential or Toxic?

Section D: The Fun Factor

  • 3 "Weird" or obscure facts.
